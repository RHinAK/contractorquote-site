Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Overview

Contractor Quote ("the App") is developed by StackStats Apps LLC ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data you enter into Contractor Quote — including client information, quotes, templates, and company settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no access to this data.

Data You Enter

The App allows you to enter the following types of information, which is stored only on your device:

  • Company information: Your company name, address, phone number, and email (used for PDF quote headers)
  • Client information: Client names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, and notes
  • Quote data: Quote titles, line items, prices, quantities, tax rates, notes, and terms
  • Templates: Custom quote templates you create

This information never leaves your device unless you explicitly share a PDF quote using the iOS share sheet.

Subscriptions & Purchases

Contractor Quote offers optional Pro subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit framework. We do not process or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Subscription options:

  • Pro Monthly: $9.99/month (7-day free trial)
  • Pro Yearly: $79.99/year (7-day free trial)

Third-Party Services

Contractor Quote does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. We do not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your data stays on your device.

When you generate and share a PDF quote, the sharing is initiated by you through iOS's native share functionality. We do not control or monitor how shared documents are transmitted.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. Since all data is stored locally, it benefits from iOS's application sandboxing and encryption.

Data Deletion

You can delete any data within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Contractor Quote is a business tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
